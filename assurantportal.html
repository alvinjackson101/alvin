Reinsurance Portal

To provide clients with a best in class/industry reinsurance reporting solution for the Assurant Global Auto (AGA) business. Clients receiving accurate, consolidated, and timely cession information through a Reinsurance Web Portal is the end goal.

The Kickoff

First meeting with Director and Manager of Innovation. Some of the many questions that were asked:


Product Description?

Pocket Geek Auto is an enterprise product that allow vehicle owners to stay connected with their vehicle regarding services, roadside, recalls, and more. Assurant customers (dealerships) has better chance to maintain customer after vehicle sells.


Product Solutions?

Vehicle owners, stay connected with your vehicle and dealerships keep your customer after a sell and stay connected to offer other services and help maintain your customer vehicle.


Product Users?

Vehicle owners and drivers. Persona age would be 25-45, people who care about their vehicle. The dealerships will be part of the application backend and using it for multiple services, promotions, and customer service.
